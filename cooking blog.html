How to Bake Perfect Cookies

Mastering Rustic Breads

🍪 Choco Chip Cookie

  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Cream butter & sugar, add eggs & mix in flour. Fold in chips. Bake at 350°F for 12 mins.

🍪 Oatmeal Raisin Cookie

  • 1 cup oats
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • ¾ cup flour
  • ½ cup raisins

Mix ingredients, scoop dough, and bake at 350°F for 10-12 minutes.

🍪 Peanut Butter Cookie

  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Mix all ingredients, shape, press with fork, and bake at 350°F for 8-10 mins.

🍞 Sourdough Bread

  • 3½ cups bread flour
  • 1 cup starter
  • 1½ cups water
  • 2 tsp salt

Mix, knead, rise 4-6 hrs, shape, rest, bake at 450°F for 45 mins.

🍞 Whole Wheat Bread

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 tsp yeast

Combine, let rise, shape into loaf, proof, and bake at 375°F for 35 mins.

🍞 French Baguette

  • 3¼ cups flour
  • 1¼ cups water
  • 1 tsp yeast
  • 1½ tsp salt

Mix, knead, let rise, shape into baguettes, proof, and bake at 450°F for 20 mins.
